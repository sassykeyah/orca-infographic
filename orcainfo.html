About Orcas

Orcas, also known as killer whales, are highly intelligent marine mammals that inhabit oceans around the world.

Threats to Orcas

Despite their powerful presence, orcas face numerous threats including pollution, habitat loss, and overfishing.

Conservation Efforts

Conservation organizations work tirelessly to protect orcas through research, advocacy, and habitat restoration projects.
